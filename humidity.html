Latitude vs. Humidity

How does Humidity change as you get closer to the equator?

The data were collected in Dec 2020 - winter of the northern hemisphere (latitude > 0o) and summer of the southern hemisphere (latitude > 0o).

Overall, the humidity seems to be higher (approximately 60% to 100%) near the equator (latitude = 0o), though there aren't any clear trend betwen the latitude and humidity in general.
